The Buffalo Blueway

  • PARTNERS
  • RESOURCES

The Buffalo Blueway is our waterway system connected by paddlesport launches which utilize existing landmarks and newly installed public art pieces to create a waterfront trail in Western New York. The Buffalo Blueway links the Niagara River Greenway to our waterfront in an expanded network of access and recreation. This Blueway-to-Greenway network forges together history, culture and nature to spur tourism, inspire healthy lifestyles and spark revitalization. The Buffalo Blueway access sites strategically connect existing assets and attractions and feature wayfinding and beacons to guide and attract patrons.
